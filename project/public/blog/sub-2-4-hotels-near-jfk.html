Does JFK Airport Have a Hotel Inside?

This is one of the most common questions from travelers researching accommodation at John F. Kennedy International Airport. The short answer: JFK does not have a hotel physically inside its terminal buildings, unlike some airports such as the Hilton connected to Boston Logan's Terminal E or the Marriott connected to Houston's IAH Terminal B.

However, JFK has an exceptional alternative: the TWA Hotel, located at Terminal 5, which is accessible directly from the AirTrain and essentially on airport grounds. For the purposes of planning a layover stay, the TWA Hotel functions as an airport hotel even though it technically requires an AirTrain or short walk to reach the terminal buildings.

Additionally, JFK has sleep pods inside certain terminals. See our full guide to sleep pods at JFK Terminal 4 for details on in-terminal rest options.

Best Hotels Near JFK Airport

The JFK hotel ecosystem is extensive. John F. Kennedy International sits in Queens, New York, surrounded by a well-developed hospitality infrastructure in the Jamaica, South Queens, and Rockaway area. Here are the standout hotels near JFK airport New York across different categories:

TWA Hotel (Premium)

The TWA Hotel at Terminal 5 is in a category of its own. Housed in the original Eero Saarinen-designed TWA Flight Center, this is one of the most architecturally significant hotels in the United States. Opened in 2019 after an extensive restoration, the TWA Hotel offers:

  • 512 rooms with floor-to-ceiling views of the tarmac
  • The Runway Rooftop Pool, an infinity pool overlooking active runways
  • Multiple dining outlets operating around the clock
  • Direct AirTrain access to all JFK terminals

Pricing is higher than standard airport hotels, but the experience is genuinely memorable. For aviation enthusiasts and design lovers, this is the definitive JFK airport hotel.

JetBlue Terminal 5 adjacency is a bonus if you are flying JetBlue, as the TWA Hotel sits next to T5 and makes connections seamless.

New York Airport Hotel Marriott JFK

The JFK Marriott is a traditional full-service hotel near JFK with shuttle service, comfortable rooms, and standard Marriott loyalty program benefits. This is the most reliable mainstream hotel option for travelers who want consistent quality at a predictable price point.

Hilton New York JFK

The Hilton JFK offers 355 rooms, a 24-hour restaurant, and complimentary shuttle service. It is a solid choice for both leisure and business travelers and one of the better-reviewed new york kennedy airport hotels in the mid-price range.

TWA Hotel: The Iconic JFK Stay

For a deeper dive on the TWA Hotel as the premier hotel john f kennedy international airport option:

The TWA Flight Center was originally completed in 1962 and served as the terminal for Trans World Airlines. After TWA merged with American Airlines in 2001, the building sat vacant for nearly two decades before RXR Realty and MCR Hotels transformed it into the TWA Hotel, which opened in May 2019.

The hotel features:

  • Rooms starting at approximately $250 per night (rates vary significantly by season)
  • A museum-quality collection of TWA memorabilia
  • Connie, a 1958 Lockheed Constellation aircraft converted into a cocktail lounge
  • Gym, spa, and event facilities

For travelers asking "are there hotels in JFK airport" while hoping for a connected on-site property, the TWA Hotel is as close as JFK gets to a true terminal hotel experience.

Where to Sleep at JFK Airport

The question of where to sleep in JFK airport or JFK airport where to sleep comes up frequently from travelers with long overnight layovers.

JFK is a 24-hour airport, but comfort varies significantly by terminal. Here is a quick guide:

In-Terminal Sleep Options

Terminal 4 is the largest terminal at JFK and handles many international carriers. Minute Suites are available at Terminal 4, providing private pay-per-hour rest rooms. See our dedicated guide to sleep pods at JFK Terminal 4 for detailed information on location, pricing, and what is included.

Terminal 5 (JetBlue) has comfortable seating and is generally quieter overnight than T4. The adjacency to the TWA Hotel means the area has 24-hour restaurant access.

Free sleeping spots at JFK: Gate seating areas throughout T4, T5, and T8 accommodate overnight travelers. Padded seating is available in most gates. JFK is not among the most comfortable free sleeping airports in the U.S., but it is manageable with the right preparation (eye mask, earplugs, travel pillow).

Hostels Near JFK Airport

For budget travelers specifically asking about hostel near JFK airport New York, the hostel inventory immediately adjacent to JFK is limited. However, Queens and Brooklyn have several well-reviewed hostels within reasonable distance via the AirTrain + subway combination.

Getting to Manhattan Hostels from JFK

The AirTrain from JFK to Jamaica Station connects to the A train, which runs directly into Manhattan 24 hours a day. Budget-conscious travelers can reach Midtown hostels in approximately 60 minutes from the terminal for approximately $11 in transit fare.

Notable hostel options accessible from JFK include properties in Manhattan's Lower East Side, Brooklyn's Williamsburg, and Jackson Heights in Queens. For overnight layovers where you genuinely need rest rather than a sightseeing base, the transit time makes a nearby airport hotel a more practical choice than a distant hostel.
